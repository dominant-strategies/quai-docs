## Fonts

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/MediaKit/Quai Network Typography.png" alt="The three fonts used in Quai Network typography, Yapari, Bai Jamjuree, and Monorama." />
</Frame>

Our brand's typography is thoughtfully selected to optimize communication. Yapari is employed for headlines, offering a bold and distinct appearance. Bai Jamjuree serves as the workhorse for body text and other essential copy, ensuring readability and coherence. Monorama is reserved for subheadings and detail text, subtly contributing to a well-balanced typographic scheme. This selection aims to facilitate clear and effective communication while maintaining a simple and understated aesthetic.

## Color Pallet

The Quai color palette is predominantly monotone, using different shades between black and white, but there is a bold red to use as an accent color. This vivid red serves as a dynamic focal point, injecting energy and vibrancy into our visual identity. Its deliberate and consistent use contributes to brand recognition and reinforces a cohesive and impactful design language for the Quai brand.
